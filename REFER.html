Referral Process

How It Works

  • Invite your friends to donate plasma – all blood types welcome!
  • We’ll contact your friend to schedule their first donation.
  • Your friend completes their first two plasma donations.
  • Your referral bonus will be available on your B Positive Visa card within 3 business days.
*First-time donor must make an appointment online in advance, and must include referring donor’s full name when they schedule their first donation appointment. Referral bonuses for walk-in new donors do not qualify.
Referral bonuses apply to first-time donors at Blood donation camp only. To be eligible, referred and referring donors must complete 2 successful donations within 45 days of first visit to our camp. Bonus will be paid to referring donor’s Blood donation camp Visa card within 3 business days of first-time donor’s 2nd complete donation. Subject to eligibility requirements.
